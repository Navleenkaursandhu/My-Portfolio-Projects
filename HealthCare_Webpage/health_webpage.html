Advancing medicine
through research

Health Care selectively partners with researches on specific diease areas, so people with those medical conditions are
eligible to contribute their records to research. By allowing these researchers to use anonymize records, Health Care is
able to provide study participants their medical record timelines for free.

We anonymize all records
We don't just redact your data by crossing out or deleting your
name. When preparing records to share with researchers, we
actually remove specific pieces of information from your
records like- lab results or -and put those into a
separate file
We structure information from records
into a single dataset
Researchers use those datasets with
the hopes of finding new breakthroughs
